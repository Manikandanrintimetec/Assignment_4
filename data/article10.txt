On March 10, 2024, a new wellness trend focusing on mindfulness and digital detox gained popularity among urban populations. The trend encourages individuals to reduce screen time and engage in activities such as meditation, yoga, and outdoor recreation.
According to a survey published on 03/11/2024, over 60% of respondents reported improved mental health after adopting these practices. Wellness coach Anita Verma emphasized the importance of maintaining a balance between technology use and personal well-being.
“Taking regular breaks from digital devices can significantly reduce stress levels,” she explained during a workshop. Many companies have also started promoting wellness programs for employees.
The trend has led to increased demand for wellness retreats and mindfulness apps. Experts believe that this shift reflects a growing awareness of mental health issues in modern society.
However, some critics argue that completely disconnecting from technology may not be practical for everyone. They suggest adopting a balanced approach instead.
Despite differing opinions, the movement continues to gain traction, with more people exploring ways to improve their overall well-being. The trend is expected to evolve further in the coming years.
